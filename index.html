Our Story

We met in 2019, at the Irish Pub, Biddy Early's in Stuttgart, Germany. We hit it off immediately, bonding on our first date at Mata Hari over our love for Science and Travel. During COVID we got the chance to get to know each other better, went on dates and cooked gnocchi with pesto together. On our first holiday, in Portugal we decided that being together 24/7 isn't that bad & we decided to move in together. We got lucky and found our best friend in each other, who never drains our social battery. And finally in August 2022, we got engaged.

Now, we are getting married!
